# Privacy Policy

**Creator OS**
**Last Updated: April 2026**
**Effective Date: April 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Creator OS ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

By using Creator OS, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Phone number (for OTP-based authentication)
- Name and email address (via Google Sign-In, if chosen)
- Profile photo

### 1.2 Social Media Data

When you voluntarily connect your social media accounts, we access data through official platform APIs:

**Instagram (via Instagram Graph API by Meta Platforms, Inc.):**
- Profile information (username, biography, profile picture, account type, media count)
- Follower and following counts
- Post content metadata (likes, comments, shares, saves, reach, impressions)
- Stories and Reels performance metrics
- Audience demographics (age ranges, gender distribution, top cities, languages)
- Hashtag performance data

**YouTube (via YouTube Data API v3 by Google LLC):**
- Channel information (channel name, subscriber count, total views, video count)
- Video performance metrics (views, watch time, click-through rate, audience retention)
- Playlist data
- Audience demographics (age, gender, geography)
- Traffic source data

We only access data from YOUR own authenticated accounts. We never access data from accounts you do not own or control.

### 1.3 Financial Information
For invoicing and tax compliance features, we collect:
- PAN (Permanent Account Number) — for TDS tracking purposes
- Bank account details (account number, IFSC code) — for future payouts
- GSTIN (GST Identification Number), if applicable
- Business expense records you voluntarily enter
- Invoice data you create through the Service

All financial data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit.

### 1.4 Usage Data
We automatically collect:
- Device information (model, operating system version, screen size)
- App usage patterns (features accessed, session duration)
- Crash reports and performance data (via Sentry)
- Anonymized product analytics (via PostHog)
- IP address (used only for geographic determination, not stored long-term)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:
- **Provide the Service**: Display your social media analytics in a unified dashboard
- **Generate media kits**: Auto-populate professional media kits with your latest stats
- **Calculate your Creator Power Score**: Our proprietary performance metric based on your public analytics
- **Generate invoices**: Create GST-compliant invoices with correct SAC codes and tax calculations
- **Track tax obligations**: Monitor TDS obligations under Sections 194R and 194J of the Income Tax Act, 1961
- **Send reminders**: Push notifications for advance tax deadlines and important updates
- **Suggest pricing**: Recommend rates for brand collaborations based on aggregated market data
- **Process payments**: Handle Pro subscription payments via Razorpay
- **Improve the Service**: Analyze anonymized usage patterns to fix bugs and improve features

We do NOT use your data for:
- Serving advertisements
- Training machine learning models on your personal data
- Creating advertising profiles
- Any purpose not explicitly described in this policy

- Within Creator OS: Go to Settings > Connected Accounts > YouTube > Disconnect

## 6. Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored on servers located in **Mumbai, India** (AWS Asia Pacific — Mumbai, ap-south-1 region)
- Social media OAuth tokens are encrypted using AES-256 and automatically refreshed before expiry
- Financial data (PAN, bank details) is encrypted at rest with access-controlled audit logs
- We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS/TLS 1.3, secure session management, parameterized database queries, and regular security reviews
- We comply with the **Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA)**
- We comply with the **Information Technology Act, 2000** and its associated rules

---

## 7. Data Retention

- **Account data**: Retained as long as your account is active
- **Social media analytics snapshots**: Retained to provide historical analytics and trend data
- **YouTube API data**: Refreshed from the API at least every 30 days as required by YouTube API Terms. Deleted within 30 days of account disconnection or deletion
- **Instagram API data**: Deleted within 30 days of account disconnection or deletion
- **Financial records** (invoices, TDS data): Retained for the statutory period required under Indian tax law (8 years from the relevant assessment year) even after account deletion
- **Upon account deletion**: All personal data (except legally required financial records) is deleted within 30 days

## 8. Data Deletion

### How to Delete Your Data

**Option 1 — Delete specific platform data:**
Go to Settings > Connected Accounts > Select platform > Disconnect
This removes all data from that platform within 30 days.

**Option 2 — Delete your entire account:**
Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account
This removes all your personal data within 30 days, except financial records required by Indian tax law.

**Option 3 — Email request:**
Send an email to creatoros.official@gmail.com with subject "Data Deletion Request"
We will process your request within 30 days and confirm via email.

### What Happens After Deletion
- All personal profile data is permanently deleted
- All social media analytics data is permanently deleted
- All OAuth tokens are revoked and deleted
- Financial records (invoices, TDS records) are retained only as required by Indian law
- Anonymized, aggregated statistics that cannot identify you may be retained
